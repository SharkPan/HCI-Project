School Life

Job bank

Discover a diverse range of both on-campus and off-campus job opportunities tailored to international students, empowering you to gain valuable work experience while studying at Trinity Western University.

Social Connection

Stay informed and engaged with upcoming events, relevant news, and a vibrant forum where you can connect with fellow students, discuss topics of interest, and forge meaningful friendships within the Trinity Western University community.

Languages

Access specialized support and resources tailored to international students facing challenges with English proficiency. From language tutoring to interactive learning materials, we’re here to help you strengthen your English skills and excel in your academic pursuits at Trinity Western University.

Success Stories

Maria, an international freshman at Trinity Western University, initially faced language barriers that hindered her academic progress. Discovering TWU International Connect, she accessed personalized language support and tutoring, gaining confidence in her English skills. Through the website’s job bank, Maria secured an on-campus job, enhancing her university experience. Today, Maria thrives academically and socially, thanks to the invaluable support provided by TWU International Connect.

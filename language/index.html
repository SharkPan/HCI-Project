Language Course

​ All programs at TLC are designed to assist students in improving their Listening, Speaking, Reading, and Writing academically and to help them to instill confidence in moving forward to the next level of learning. In addition, these Programs will equip students with the language skills that are essential for them to succeed the future undergraduate/graduate studies at the post-secondary institutions they plan to pursue.

Academic English Study Program (AES) – For Future Undergraduate Studies

To achieve the best result in teaching and learning, undergraduate language preparation programs at TLC are organized to teach academic English skills in a progressive approach within the context of the content area. This Modular Academic System (MAS) of Learning is composed of four progressive levels. Each level will be taught through the lens of 3 Academic Content Areas–Humanities and Social Sciences (MAS-H), Sciences and Math (MAS-S), and Professional Studies (MAS-P). Each level is composed of three 5-week learning and assessment sessions; students are therefore able to start the program every five weeks. In addition to the weekly 20-hours direct learning, students will attend daily Supervised Assignment Session to supplement in-class learning.

This program has been approved by the Private Training Institutions Branch (PTIB) of the Ministry of Advanced Education, Skills & Training.

AES Level 1

For learners at the basic level of English. The three modules include

  • 1-S (Structural Science: Architecture)
  • 1-P (Business and Technology)
  • 1-H (Humanities: Psychology

AES Level 2

Continues teaching learners at the basic level. The three modules include

  • 2-P (Business: Money and E-Commerce)
  • 2-H (Cultural Anthropology)
  • 2-S (Engineering)

AES Level 3

For pre-intermediate learners. The three modules include

  • 3-S (Bioethics)
  • 3-P (Business Studies)
  • 3-H (History)

AES Level 4

For intermediate learners. The three modules include

  • 4-H (Linguistics)
  • 4-P (Business Ethics)
  • 4-S (Earth Science)

Pre-Graduate Study Program (PGS) – For Future Graduate Studies

PGS Level 1

This level is delivered with the combination of TLC Academic English course and two TWU BA Leadership credit courses.

PGS Level 2

This level is delivered with the combination of TLC Academic English course and three TWU BA Leadership credit courses.

Students who successfully complete the PGS (Pre-Graduate Study) Level-1 & 2 and meet the GPA requirements in the specified credit courses will be admissible into the TWU graduate programs requiring an IELTS 7.0 English Language proficiency.

PGS Program does not require approval by the Private Training Institutions Branch (PTIB) of the Ministry of Advanced Education, Skills & Training. As such, PTIB did not review this program.

CAEL Test and TLC In-House Placement Test

A key component of the TLC system is the assessment of learners into appropriate language learning categories. To assess the English proficiency of incoming students, TLC works closely with TWU on offering The CAEL (Canadian Academic English Language) Placement Assessment. Additionally, TLC-In-House Placement Test is available multiple times a semester to provide more chances for potential students to write the test.

Tutorials

TLC offers a unique tutoring system to provide academic support to students. Utilizing a Supervised Assignment Session and tutoring by appointment, TLC Program Assistant and Academic Coordinator will work closely with each instructor to keep track of the learning progress of students while offering specific assistance to students as needed or upon request of the instructor. Students who are late or absent due to illness or unexpected conditions are encouraged to book appointments with instructors and the tutor to make up the missed sessions.

Welcome to the Writing Centre!

We are here to assist you with academic writing assignments in any course. You don't have to have your paper finished to come to the Writing Centre. You can bring just the topic or question, and we can help get you started. Come discuss your writing at any stage! We will not edit or proofread for you, but we will help you to identify writing trends and revise your work.
